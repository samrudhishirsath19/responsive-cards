Book Categories

Adventure

Explore thrilling journeys, daring quests, and epic expeditions full of excitement and danger.Bilbo Baggins, a reluctant hobbit, embarks on an unexpected adventure with a group of dwarves to reclaim their homeland from the dragon Smaug. Along the way, he faces challenges, uncovers treasure, and discovers his own courage, setting the stage for the epic tale that unfolds in The Lord of the Rings series.

Mystery

Dive into mind-bending puzzles and suspensful tales that will keep you guessing until the end.Harvard symbologist Robert Langdon is thrust into a thrilling mystery when a curator at the Louvre is found dead, leaving behind cryptic clues. As Langdon teams up with cryptologist Sophie Neveu, they uncover a conspiracy that links historical secrets, art, and religion, racing against time to solve the puzzle before it’s too late.

Fantasy

Enter magical realms filled with legendary heroes, mythical creatures, and ancient prophecies.Eleven-year-old Harry Potter discovers that he is a wizard and is invited to attend Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. As he makes new friends and learns magic, Harry uncovers a dark secret about his past and faces a powerful enemy determined to return to power.

Science Fiction

Discover futuristic technologies, alien civilizations, and imaginative possibilities beyond Earth.In a distant future, Paul Atreides, heir to the noble House Atreides, becomes embroiled in a struggle for control of the desert planet Arrakis, the only source of the universe’s most valuable substance, "spice." Amid political intrigue and ecological challenges, Paul must navigate prophecy, power, and survival on the harsh world of Dune.

Biography

Read about inspiring lives and the extraordinary stories of people who changed the world.This poignant and powerful memoir is the account of Anne Frank, a Jewish teenager who hid with her family during the Holocaust in Nazi-occupied Netherlands. Through her diary, Anne shares her thoughts, dreams, and struggles, offering a personal and emotional perspective on fear, hope, and resilience in the face of unimaginable hardship.
